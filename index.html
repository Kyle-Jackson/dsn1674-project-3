Come taste our award winning pizza!

Sammy's Restaurant aims to bring a fresh, unique spin on how you order, eat & enjoy your pizza. We allow you to customize your pizza according to your personal taste instead of insisting on specialty pizzas where no substitutions are allowed. Come in and experience both exquisite pizza unlike any you've ever tried! Enjoy a Dunrobin, Firefighter themed dining experience.

We take great pride in making our delicious pizza with fresh ingredients, lots of cheese and our famous homemade sauce.
